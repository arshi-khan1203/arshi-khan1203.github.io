The LLM Training Pipeline

From Raw Data to Deployed Model — 6 Key Stages

💾
01

Data Collection

Web crawls, books, code repositories, and licensed datasets. Petabytes of raw text gathered from diverse sources.

🔍
02

Data Filtering

Deduplication, quality filtering, and toxic content removal. Only high-quality curated text proceeds to training.

🧠
03

Pre-Training

Self-supervised learning on massive corpus using next-token prediction. Most compute-intensive stage.

⚙
04

Fine-Tuning (SFT)

Supervised learning on curated instruction-response pairs to shape model behavior for specific tasks.

✓
05

RLHF Alignment

Human feedback ranks outputs. A reward model guides further training toward helpful, safe responses.

☁
06

Deployment

Safety testing, API integration, infrastructure scaling, and ongoing performance monitoring.

Key Takeaways

What We Learned About LLM Training

01

Scale Demands Massive Resources

Training frontier LLMs requires thousands of specialized GPUs, months of runtime, and investments exceeding $50-100M. This barrier limits who can build foundation models.

02

Data Quality Drives Performance

More data is not always better. Curated, filtered, high-quality datasets consistently outperform larger but noisier corpora in producing reliable model outputs.

03

RLHF is Essential for Safety

Human feedback alignment transforms a raw language model into a helpful, safe, and usable product. It is iterative, expensive, and ongoing throughout the model lifecycle.

04

Energy Costs Are Real and Growing

The environmental footprint of AI training is significant and increasing. Responsible AI development requires transparency about energy use and investment in cleaner infrastructure.
